# RTGS.global Product Facts

## What is RTGS.global?

RTGS.global is a real-time gross settlement network that connects payment service providers, fintechs, and financial institutions for instant cross-border payments without the traditional correspondent banking infrastructure.

### Core Concept
Instead of routing payments through chains of correspondent banks (which adds days and fees), RTGS.global enables direct settlement between network members in seconds.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### "How do you make money?"
Transaction-based pricing. We charge per payment, with volume-based pricing tiers. No hidden fees.

### "What currencies do you support?"
Growing list of currencies across our corridor network. Contact us for current coverage.

### "How long does integration take?"
Basic integration typically takes 2-3 weeks. Full production rollout depends on testing requirements.

### "Do you replace our banking relationships?"
No - we complement existing relationships. Most customers use us for specific corridors while maintaining their bank partnerships.

### "How do you handle compliance?"
We work with licensed funds controllers in each market. They handle local regulatory requirements. We provide ISO20022-aligned messaging and support AML/sanctions screening.

### "What if there's an issue with a payment?"
Real-time visibility means you see payment status immediately. Our support team handles exceptions. Full audit trail for investigation.

### "Can we white-label this for our customers?"
Yes - you can offer RTGS-powered payments under your brand. API supports white-label implementations.
