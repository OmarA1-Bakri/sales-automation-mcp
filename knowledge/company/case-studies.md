# RTGS.global Case Studies

## 1. UK PSP Treasury Optimization

**Company Profile:**
- Industry: Payment Service Provider
- Size: 200 employees, €500M annual volume
- Region: UK/Europe
- Pain Point: Liquidity management, working capital tied up

**Challenge:**
Pre-funded €8M across 6 correspondent accounts to support cross-border payments. Treasury team of 3 spending 60% of time managing cash positions across multiple corridors.

**Solution:**
Implemented RTGS.global just-in-time funding:
- Real-time settlement eliminated pre-funding requirements
- Single dashboard for global liquidity visibility
- 24/7 automated position management

**Results:**
- **60% cost reduction** in correspondent banking fees
- **40% improvement** in working capital efficiency
- **€12M freed** from pre-funded accounts
- Treasury team refocused on strategic initiatives

**Best Quote:**
> "We went from managing 6 correspondent relationships to one settlement partner. The operational complexity reduction alone justified the switch." — Head of Treasury

**Use When:**
- Prospect mentions liquidity challenges
- Treasury persona (Head of Treasury, VP Finance, CFO)
- Company size 100-500 employees
- Cross-border payment volume

## 2. Singapore Fintech Global Expansion

**Company Profile:**
- Industry: Fintech
- Size: 120 employees
- Region: APAC (Singapore HQ)
- Pain Point: Licensing barriers, time to market

**Challenge:**
Wanted to expand from 3 markets (Singapore, Hong Kong, Australia) to 18 markets across APAC and Europe. Traditional licensing approach estimated at 2+ years and $5M+ in regulatory costs.

**Solution:**
Partnered with RTGS.global's licensed funds controllers:
- Instant access to pre-licensed corridors
- Local expertise in compliance and settlement
- Focus resources on customer acquisition

**Results:**
- **15 new markets in 3 months** (vs 2+ years traditional)
- **$4M saved** in licensing costs
- **First revenue in new markets within 6 weeks**
- Now processing $200M/month across network

**Best Quote:**
> "We went from a 3-market company to an 18-market company in one quarter. Our competitors are still filling out license applications." — CEO

**Use When:**
- Prospect mentions expansion plans
- Recent funding signal
- Business development or partnerships persona
- Growth-stage fintech

## 3. Middle East Payment Operator - Settlement Speed

**Company Profile:**
- Industry: Payment Operator
- Size: 400 employees
- Region: Middle East (UAE HQ)
- Pain Point: Slow settlement, customer complaints

**Challenge:**
Processing $50M/month through SWIFT with T+2 settlement times. Customers complaining about delays, losing deals to faster competitors. Customer NPS at 32.

**Solution:**
Migrated APAC corridor to RTGS.global:
- Real-time settlement (seconds, not days)
- 24/7 operation (no banking hours constraints)
- Full transparency on payment status

**Results:**
- **Settlement time: T+2 days → 12 seconds**
- **NPS improved by 25 points** (32 → 57)
- **15% increase in transaction volume** from happy customers
- **45% cost reduction** on APAC corridor

**Best Quote:**
> "Our customers used to call asking where their money was. Now they call to say thank you." — Head of Operations

**Use When:**
- Prospect mentions settlement delays
- Operations or payments persona
- Customer experience concerns
- Competitive pressure on speed
